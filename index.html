Before you upload

  • Fill the frame with the sheet; avoid heavy glare and blur.
  • Handwriting and faint marks are error-prone. Always cross-check on the review page.
  • Extraction runs in the cloud; do not upload sheets you are not allowed to share.
  • iPhone photos are compressed automatically so they stay within upload limits.

Privacy

  • Images are sent to a third-party AI vision provider for extraction, then removed from this server.
  • Your session keeps a private copy of the extracted match JSON on the server when storage is available.
  • If the host does not provide storage, that data stays only in this browser.
  • Do not upload sheets you are not allowed to share.
